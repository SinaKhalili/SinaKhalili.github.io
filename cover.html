Yet Another Cover Letter Generator

What's Yet Another Cover Letter Generator?

YACLG is a cover letter generator that attempts to match your skills with the skills within the requirements of the job posting.


How to use:

Simply copy-paste the job requirements (don't worry about formating) into the required field. Add your information to the JSON object (NOTE skill titles in lowercase). For every skill try to write a few sentences about how you have that skill. This will be aggregated to the main paragraph of the cover letter for skills which are both in the requirements as well as your user object. The template uses <company>, <name>, <lname>, <major>, <matchedskills> (the list of skills which you match, comma seperated), and <skillsentences> (the aggregation of the matched skill sentences).
